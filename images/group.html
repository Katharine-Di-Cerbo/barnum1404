THE WORKROOM 

Working with Makkas Drapery Workroom is simple, no matter how complex your design. We work closely with you throughout the process to ensure that your project is delivered in a timely manner.

&nbsp;

Our Process:

  1. Schedule an appointment for measurements.
  2. Review designs and details with you.
  3. Provide an estimate for services.
  4. An installation date is scheduled.
  5. Your design is fabricated.
  6. Design is completed and installed.
